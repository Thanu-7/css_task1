Scroll down to see the sticky header in action.

The sticky header uses position: fixed to stay pinned to the top of the page when you scroll. It has a logo on the left and horizontal navigation links on the right, arranged with flexbox. Padding keeps things spaced nicely, and a subtle shadow adds depth. A top margin on the main content prevents it from hiding behind the header. This keeps the navigation always visible and easy to use while scrolling.
